# Shreya Shankar / Shreya Shankar

## English

Shreya Shankar is a key voice on evaluation, data quality, and the interaction between human criteria and LLM systems.

### Recommended entry points

1. Co-author of LLM Evals: Everything You Need to Know
   - Link: https://hamel.dev/blog/posts/evals-faq/
   - Why it matters: Strong practical guidance on how evaluation criteria emerge through annotation and review.

2. Personal site
   - Link: https://www.sh-reya.com/
   - Why it matters: Good place to track her current work on data systems, agents, and evaluation-related research.

### Core lessons

- Evaluation criteria are often discovered during annotation, not fully known in advance
- Good evals depend on data work, not just prompts and dashboards
- Agent evaluation benefits from thinking like a data systems engineer
