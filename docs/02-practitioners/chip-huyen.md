# Chip Huyen / Chip Huyen

## English

Chip Huyen is useful in this repository not because she offers one eval framework, but because she consistently connects evaluation to production system design.

### What to learn from her work

- evaluation must reflect product use cases
- latency, cost, and reliability belong in the same conversation as quality
- agent systems should be judged by whether they complete useful work, not whether they look impressive in demos

### Recommended resources

1. AI Engineering: Building Applications with Foundation Models
   - Why it matters: strong framing for production AI systems and evaluation trade-offs

2. Agents
   - Link: https://huyenchip.com/2025/01/07/agents.html
   - Why it matters: a clear systems view on what agents are, what makes them difficult, and why evaluation needs to focus on end-to-end behavior
